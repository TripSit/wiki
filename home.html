This is the TripSit community wiki.
Our goal is to collect, maintain, and share reliable harm reduction knowledge in a way that is accessible and collaborative.

We currently have 491 pages covering a wide range of topics including substance info, moderation policy, tools and guides, mental health, and community documentation.

All users are welcome to contribute! Log in with your TripSit account using the link in the sidebar to edit or create pages. For small corrections and typos, feel free to make changes directly. For larger or structural changes, please fork the project and submit a pull request via GitHub:

github.com/tripsit/wiki

Want to help?

  • Create an account and make changes!
  • For larger suggestions, open an issue on GitHub.

Code of Conduct

All contributions to the wiki must follow our community values: accuracy, empathy, non-judgment, and respect for lived experience. This is a collaborative and harm-reduction-first space.
